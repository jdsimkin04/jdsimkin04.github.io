Data and analytics leader with a PhD in epidemiology. I build and lead across applied AI and ML, population-scale data systems, and the research and reporting they make possible, mostly in healthcare and other high-stakes settings.

My recent work is applied AI: NLP and language-model systems running in production inside a provincial health system. But the through-line is broader. I transform the data systems health care depends on, turn what they hold into evidence people act on, and lead the teams that do both. My training in epidemiology keeps careful inference and scientific rigor underneath all of it.
